What should users see while the 3D model loads?

The model currently pops in suddenly. Pick the loading treatment that fits the Glimpse aesthetic.

A

Centered spinner / pulse

A minimal animated dot or ring in the center of the canvas. Clean, unobtrusive. Disappears once the model is ready.

B

Silhouette skeleton

A dark ghost outline of the device shape fades in first, then dissolves into the real model. Gives spatial context while loading.

C

Fade-in on ready

Canvas starts transparent (or with a subtle gradient). When the model is fully loaded it fades in smoothly over ~0.6s. No placeholder — just a clean appearance.

D

Full-page loader gate

A brief full-screen overlay (matching site background) with a logo or wordmark and progress bar. Lifts away once assets are ready. Most branded, most control.
