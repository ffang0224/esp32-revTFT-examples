How should the device look?

The dark matte case sits on a transparent canvas over your site's background. Pick the rendering direction.

A

Studio clean

HDRI environment map for physically accurate reflections. Soft contact shadow pooling under the device. Apple-product-page energy — precise, premium, minimal. The dark case gets subtle highlight edges from the environment.

B

Cinematic with glow

Bloom post-processing on bright highlights and the e-ink screen edges. Subtle depth-of-field. More dramatic, editorial feel — like a product film rather than a product page.

C

Current lights + contact shadow

Keep the existing warm key / cool rim directional lights as-is, but add a soft contact shadow beneath the device and ground the float animation. Lowest risk, targeted improvement only.
