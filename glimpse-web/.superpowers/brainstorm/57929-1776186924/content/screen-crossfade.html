How should the e-ink screen transition between images?

Currently it's an instant swap. What should the transition feel like?

A

Dissolve / opacity crossfade

The new image fades in over the old one over ~0.4s. Smooth and invisible — feels like the screen is just "updating."

B

E-ink flash → reveal

A brief white flash (like real e-ink displays do when refreshing), then the new image appears. Authentic to the hardware behavior.

C

Wipe / scan line

New image sweeps in from top to bottom, mimicking a slow e-ink refresh scan. Most literal simulation of actual e-ink behavior.
