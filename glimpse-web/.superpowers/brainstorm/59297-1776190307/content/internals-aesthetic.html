What should the internals look like?

PCB, pins, NeoPixel LEDs, battery, screws — all visible through the frosted shell. Pick the overall tone.

A

Realistic hardware

PCB is PCB-green (#1a7a3c) with metallic traces. Screws + pins + carabiner are brushed silver. NeoPixel LEDs glow their accent color (■ #2997ff). Battery is matte black. Looks like an actual device teardown.

B

Monochrome dark hardware

All internals stay dark/near-black but with distinct material finishes: PCB is matte dark green, metal parts are dark gunmetal, LEDs still emit the blue accent. Coherent with the site's dark palette — the transparency is the statement, not the colors.

C

Accent-driven

Most internals are a single neutral tone (dark grey/charcoal), but NeoPixel LEDs glow brightly in the site's blue accent (■ #2997ff) and the carabiner gets a warm gold metallic. Two accent pops against a dark neutral field.
