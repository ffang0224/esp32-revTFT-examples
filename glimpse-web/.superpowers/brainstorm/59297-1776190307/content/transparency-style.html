How transparent should the case be?

The case shell (case_upper + case_lower) becomes see-through, revealing the interior. Pick the treatment.

A

Ghost / barely-there

Very low opacity (~10–15%). Case is almost invisible — just a faint outline suggests the shell. Interior components dominate. Futuristic, minimal.

B

Smoked glass

Medium opacity (~35–45%) with slight refraction feel. You can clearly see the internals but the case still has mass and presence. Like a smoked acrylic panel.

C

Frosted / translucent

High opacity (~60–70%) with roughness so light scatters through. Internals are visible but softened/blurred — silhouettes rather than detail. Like frosted polycarbonate.
